Team Participation

Our team is participating in the Formula Students competition, a global motorsport competition. The goal of each team is to design, analyze and build a single-seater that meets the requirements of the FSAE regulations.The competition is divided into 2 categories: static events, which include business presentation, cost analysis and design evaluation, while dynamic events, which include endurance racing, efficiency, autocross racing, acceleration test and skid pad.
